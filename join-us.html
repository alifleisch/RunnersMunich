JOIN A COMMUNITY TO BECOME A STRONGER INDIVIDUAL

We believe that each of our runners is only as strong as the community around them it's by coming together collectively that we're best able to achieve individual growth and success. Guidance, support, motivation, inspiration - these are the qualities that drive our community forward. Detach yourself from the maddening need go faster during every run: it is these qualities that sets adidas Runners apart from the rest, forging a true sense of belonging that resonates long after a run has ended.

VOLUNTEER WITH US

Join the Runners Munich family as a volunteer and be part of a community that celebrates health, wellness, and the joy of running. Our volunteers are the backbone of our events, helping to create a supportive and encouraging environment for runners of all levels. Whether you're cheering on participants, aiding at water stations, or assisting with event setup, your contribution will make a significant impact. Volunteering with us is not only a chance to give back but also a fantastic opportunity to meet like-minded individuals, gain new experiences, and enjoy the vibrant atmosphere of our running events. Plus, you'll receive special volunteer perks, including event t-shirts, appreciation parties, and discounts on future events. Come, lend a hand, and experience the rewarding feeling of supporting your fellow runners and the broader Munich running community.
