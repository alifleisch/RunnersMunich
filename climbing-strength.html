A HOLISTIC APPROACH

The holistic approach of Munich Runners is built around three performance-focused pillars: MINDSET, MOVEMENT AND STRETCHING. And to ensure no stone is left unturned to make the best version of you a reality, these are supported by a fifth: GEAR. We offer a variety of strength and climbing sessions with the aim of increasing our Munich Runners' quality of MOVEMENT. Dedicated coaches are on hand at our Bases to advise on what, when and how to train. We improve runners' STRETCHING thanks to expert coaching on how to avoid hypertension and injuries. Finally, by focusing on MINDSET exercises, our coaches also help sharpen the most powerful instruments in a runner's toolkit - their mental fortitude and sense of self-belief.

BECOME MORE FLEXIBLE AND STRONG WITH OUR CLIMBERS

Our dedicated climbing, strength and flexibility coaches, specializes in helping athletes enhance their range of motion and prevent injuries through targeted strength and stretching routines. Their expertise in power and dynamic stretching techniques has made them an invaluable asset to our team, ensuring that our runners and climbers maintain optimal performance.

BE PART OF SOMETHING THAT HAS AN IMPACT BEYOND RUNNING

Embark on a journey to elevate your climbing skills with our comprehensive training programs at Runners Munich. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced climber, our tailored programs are designed to meet your individual needs, ensuring a balanced development of skills that are crucial for conquering the most challenging routes.

At the heart of our climbing programs are our highly skilled and experienced coaches. Each coach brings a wealth of knowledge, not only from personal climbing achievements but also from a deep understanding of sports science and effective training methodologies. Their expertise ensures that every athlete receives personalized attention, guidance, and encouragement, fostering an environment where you can thrive and surpass your expectations.

Our programs cater to all levels, starting with foundational courses for beginners that focus on climbing basics, safety, and etiquette. As climbers progress, intermediate and advanced training sessions introduce more complex techniques and strategies, including bouldering, route reading, and efficient movement on the wall.

Understanding the physical demands of climbing, our programs emphasize specialized strength and conditioning. Climbers engage in targeted exercises that enhance grip strength, core stability, and endurance, ensuring a well-rounded physical preparation that translates directly into improved climbing performance.
