ABOUT US

Welcome to Runners Munich, a community-driven sports organization dedicated to promoting health, wellness, and camaraderie through running. Founded with a passion for running, strength training and climbing that courses through the heart of Munich, our mission is to offer accessible, enjoyable, and diverse programs that cater to all ages and skill levels.

At the heart of Runners Munich is the belief that sports has the power to transform lives, build strong communities, and create lasting friendships. Whether you're here to improve your fitness, take on new challenges, or simply enjoy the beauty of Munich, Runners Munich provides a supportive and vibrant environment to do so.

Our offerings range from beginner-friendly training in the English Garden, challenging sprints on the track, to adventurous trail runs along the Isar and in the Bavarian Alps, all led by our passionate and certified instructors. We also partner with Boulderwelt Munich East to provide climbing training sessions, complemented by strength and conditioning workouts in the Hofgarten. These diverse activities are designed not only to enhance your physical capabilities but also to enrich your outdoor experience and connection with nature. Beyond physical activities, we're committed to fostering a sense of belonging and achievement among our members, encouraging everyone to reach their personal best.

Join us at Runners Munich to embark on a journey of fitness, fun, and friendship. Together, we'll explore the beauty of Munich and its surroundings, one stride at a time.

OUR STORY

Runners Munich began as a small group of running enthusiasts who shared a deep love for the sport and a dream to bring people together through the joy of running. What started as informal meet-ups in the English Garden quickly grew into a community, bound by a shared passion for wellness, adventure, and the great outdoors.

Our founders, a diverse team of athletes, and coaches, were inspired by the transformative power of sports. They saw running not just as a physical activity, but as a way to connect with others, explore the landscapes of Munich, and challenge oneself in new and exciting ways. With this vision, Runners Munich was officially established, aiming to create a welcoming space for all, regardless of age or fitness level.

Today, Runners Munich stands as a testament to the power of community and the shared pursuit of health and happiness. We're more than just a sports organization; we're a family that supports and uplifts each other, every step of the way. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to inspiring more individuals to join us, embrace the active lifestyle, and experience the joy and fulfillment that comes from being part of our community.
